About Us

Rooted in Ranchi.
Reaching the World.

Founded in 2000, S.K. Publishing Company is headquartered in the heart of Morabadi, Ranchi, Jharkhand. We are committed to sharing a variety of voices and stories with readers around the globe.

Driven by a love for quality and creativity, we focus on books across numerous genres — fiction, non-fiction, children's literature, philosophy, anthropology, environmental science, and academic works. Our goal is to inspire, educate, and entertain through the written word, leaving a meaningful mark on the literary world.
